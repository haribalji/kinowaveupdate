About Us

In a world grappling with the unrestrained use of fossil fuels and a looming 50% surge in global energy demand by 2030, we, as a company, have embraced the profound need for change. Committed to a vision of a cleaner and more sustainable future, we are driven by empathy for our planet and the well-being of generations to come. While the concept of wave energy has been studied for centuries, an astounding 1200 terawatt-hours (TWh) of this potential remains untapped, owing to the absence of efficient wave energy extraction systems. This untapped resource serves as the fuel for our unwavering determination. Our mission is clear: to revolutionize the energy landscape by developing innovative wave energy extraction technologies. We aim to not only confront the pressing energy challenges but also reduce our dependence on fossil fuels, mitigate environmental threats, and secure a sustainable legacy. Recognizing that existing renewable energy sources have made only partial progress in addressing these challenges, we are poised to lead the charge in harnessing the vast potential of wave energy, illuminating a cleaner, more sustainable world for all.

What Do We Do ?

Kinowave is committed to harnessing energy from oceans through locally available eco-friendly technology that does not disturb the ecosystem. Our human resources and technology will create superior products and services in ocean power, thereby contributing to growing energy needs.We also support the government, locals, and fishermen through our highly adoptable technology that leads to a self-sustaining future.

Why Wave Energy ?

Covering more than 70% of the Earth's surface, oceans represent the predominant feature of our planet. Remarkably, they hold an energy potential of 2 terawatts, making them a formidable source of power. Among renewable sources, wave energy stands out for its exceptional energy density. This realization has ignited our exploration of this vast and promising resource.
